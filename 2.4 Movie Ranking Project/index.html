The best movies according to Deya

My top 3 movies of all time.

For Roseanna

This is a touching story for a man ready to do anything to save the life of all people in his village, because there are only 3 greve yard lefts in the church cemetery. His wife is sick and on the verdige of death, and her diying wish is to be buried in the church cemetery where their dother is berried too. The passion that he shows to preserve the people lifes just so his wife can have her whish fulfilled is admirable.

Game of thrones

Game of thrones is a fantasy drama series that has captivated audiences worldwide. I love that George R.R. Martin was not affraid to create a complex and very well developed character, just so you can see them die in the next moment. Also rather then showing the beautiful peacful world, he create something more realistic, even though using mistical creatures and powers, because his characters are complex, nothing is black and white, and the world is not perfect. I also love the fact that the story is not only about the fight for the throne, but also about the fight for survival, and how people can be good or bad depending on the situation they are in.

My Family

Even though it is a britcom and not a 'serious' movie, I love this show because regardless of what mood I am and what happens in my life, every time I watch it (and it is quite rewatchable ;) it can always make me laugh and forget everything else.
